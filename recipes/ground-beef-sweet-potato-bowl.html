High-Protein Ground Beef Sweet Potato Bowl

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 4.5 ounces 96/4 lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup Daisy 4% Cottage Cheese
  • 1/2 cup peeled sweet potato, cut into uneven bite-size cubes
  • 1/4 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon avocado, diced into irregular pieces
  • Kinder's steak seasoning, to taste
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Tajín seasoning, to taste
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Toss the sweet potato cubes with the olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Air-fry the sweet potato for 10 to 14 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through, until browned outside and tender inside.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the ground beef in a nonstick skillet over medium heat, breaking it into naturally uneven crumbles.
  5. When the beef is mostly browned, season it with Kinder's steak seasoning.
  6. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of water and stir until the seasoning coats the beef and the water cooks down.
  7. Add the ground beef and roasted sweet potato to a bowl.
  8. Spoon the cottage cheese loosely beside the beef and sweet potato rather than shaping it into a perfect scoop.
  9. Top with the irregularly diced avocado and a light sprinkle of Tajín.

40/400 Notes

  • Adding the water after the beef begins browning keeps very lean beef moist without preventing browning.
  • Cut the sweet potato into slightly uneven pieces for natural texture, but keep them similar enough to cook evenly.
  • Measure the olive oil because even a small amount changes the calorie total.
  • Nutrition is approximate and may vary by beef, cottage cheese, avocado, and seasoning brands.
